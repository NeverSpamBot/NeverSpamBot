NeverSpamBot

Your go-to tool for eliminating spam while keeping your Telegram groups clean and enjoyable.

Why Use NeverSpamBot?

  • 99% Spam Detection Rate: Effectively removes nearly all spam with minimal false positives.
  • No Captcha Needed: Stops spammers without frustrating your real users.
  • Non-Intrusive: Posts minimal notifications and only when absolutely necessary.
  • Smart Filters: Learns and improves over time, adapting to new spam tactics.

How It Works

  1. Tracks New Members: Filters messages from users who joined after the bot was added.

Keeping Your Group Safe

NeverSpamBot monitors and filters:

  • Suspicious Content: Links, financial offers, unsolicited invites, and more.
  • Media Filters: Photos, videos, and emojis based on admin settings.

What to Do If You’re Flagged: Simply write a normal, non-suspicious message, or wait for an admin to approve you. The bot is designed to adapt and learn that you're a real participant.
